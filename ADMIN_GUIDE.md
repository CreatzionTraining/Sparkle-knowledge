## üìñ How to Create a Blog Post

### Step 1: Fill in the Title
Enter a catchy, descriptive title for your blog post.

### Step 2: Write a Short Description
Write 1-2 sentences summarizing what your blog post is about.

### Step 3: Write Your Content
1. Click in the content area
2. Use the formatting buttons to style your text:
   - Select text and click **Bold** to make it bold
   - Click **H2 Heading** to add a main heading
   - Click **Paragraph** to add regular text
   - Click **List** to create bullet points

### Step 4: Add Optional Details
- Select a category from the dropdown
- Enter your name as the author
- Add estimated reading time
- Upload a featured image (optional)

### Step 5: Preview Your Post
Scroll down to see a live preview of how your blog post will look.

### Step 6: Publish!
Click the big **üöÄ PUBLISH POST** button to publish your blog post.

## üí° Tips for Great Blog Posts

1. **Use Headings** - Break up your content with H2 and H3 headings
2. **Keep Paragraphs Short** - 3-4 sentences per paragraph
3. **Use Lists** - Make information easy to scan
4. **Add Bold Text** - Highlight important points
5. **Write Clear Titles** - Make it obvious what the post is about

## üé® Formatting Examples

## üîß Troubleshooting

**Q: The Publish button is disabled**
A: Make sure you've filled in the Title, Description, and Content fields.

**Q: My formatting isn't showing up**
A: Make sure you're using the formatting buttons correctly. Select text first, then click the formatting button.

**Q: Can I edit published posts?**
A: Currently, you can only create new posts. Edit functionality coming soon!
